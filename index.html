About Us &Our Mission

Our mission is to support upcoming womxn and their teams on their journey to success. WomxnOrg is a community of ambitious people looking to connect with the startup world. We believe that one day every founder out there will be powerful enough to change the world.

We organize epic events, interview entrepreneurs, share helpful resources and inspirations, connect our community with great mentors and career opportunities. Join us and meet inspiring people with amazing stories and lessons to share.
